Travel and Stay

A map of locations for both the 4th and 6th of September

Travelling

If you don't have a car or accommodation near to the venues, public transport is viable.
All 3 venue locations have a nearby station that can be reached by a SWR train from Waterloo (or any proceeding station e.g. Clapham Junction)
Many people have cars and will try to pick up people for short distances, we will try to facilitate this to the best of our abilities, however public transport/ubers are viable alternatives.


Hindu Wedding

The Hindu Wedding will be held at Nakshatra Hall, Feltham.
BY TRAIN: The nearest station is Feltham station. From there you may get the 235 bus for 15 min or an Uber for 10 min to the venue.
BY CAR: There is free parking onsite at Nakshatra Hall

Civil

The Civil Wedding will be held in the Loggia Room at York House, Twickenham.
BY TRAIN: The nearest station is Twickenham stationw hich is an 8 min walk from York House.
BY CAR: There is no parking onsite, the nearest paid car parks are: York House Car Park and Arragon Car Park.

Reception

The reception will be held at Honeymoond Banqueting in Hounslow.
BY TRAIN: The nearest stations are Hounslow underground on Picadilly and Hounslow station, each under 10 min walk to Honeymoon Banqueting
BY CAR: There is free parking opposite the hall which is free after 6:30pm.

Where to Stay

If you would like to stay in the area, there are a few hotels nearby.
There are hotels near each of the venues, but we recommend staying in Hounslow and travelling to the other venues, as that is the location of the Reception (where drinking is involved!)
