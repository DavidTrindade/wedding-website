A map of locations for both the 4th and 6th of September

Travelling


All the venues have accessible and nearby parking and also viable public transport using SWR trains through Waterloo/Clapham Junction.


Hindu Wedding


The Hindu Wedding will be held at Nakshatra Hall, Feltham (next to the Garden Centre).
BY TRAIN: The nearest station is Feltham station. From there you may get the 235 bus for 15 min or an Uber for 10 min to the venue.
BY CAR: There is free parking onsite at Nakshatra Hall.


Civil


The Civil Wedding will be held in the Loggia Room at York House, Twickenham.
BY TRAIN: The nearest station is Twickenham station which is an 8 min walk from York House.
BY CAR: The nearest paid car parks are: York House Car Park (29 spaces) and Arragon Car Park (437 spaces).


Reception


The reception will be held at Honeymoon Banqueting Hall in Hounslow.
BY TRAIN: The nearest stations are Hounslow Central (Piccadilly Line) and Hounslow train station, each under 10 min walk to the venue.
BY CAR: There is parking opposite the hall which is free after 6:30pm.


Where to Stay


If you would like to stay in the area, there are a few hotels nearby.
There are hotels near each of the venues, but we recommend staying in Hounslow and travelling to the other venues, as that is the location of the Reception (where drinking is involved!)
