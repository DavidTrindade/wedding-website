Travelling

If you don't have a car or accommodation near to the venues, public transport is viable.
All 3 venue locations have a nearby station that can be reached by a SWR train from Waterloo (or any proceeding station e.g. Clapham Junction)
Many people have cars and will try to pick up people for short distances, we will try to facilitate this to the best of our abilities, however public transport/ubers are vialble alternatives.

Hindu Wedding

The Hindu Wedding will be held in Feltham.
Ideally people will be picking up guests from the station, but if no-one is available there is a bus that goes to the venue in 15 minutes.

Civil & Reception

The Civil Wedding will be held in Twickenham.
The venue is an 8 minute walk from the station.

The Reception will be held in Hounslow.
We will be travelling from the Civil Wedding to the Reception immediately after the ceremony. If you cannot find a ride with anyone, there is a 25 minute bus/train available.
We will wait for you do not worry.

Where to Stay

If you would like to stay in the area, there are a few hotels nearby.
There are hotels near each of the venues, but we recommend staying in Hounslow and travelling to the other venues, as that is the location of the Reception, where drinking is involved.
